Who We Are

Our Story

A passion for food, rooted in Nairobi, inspired by Italy

Est. 2018

From a Dream to a Destination

Savoria was born in 2018 when Chef Amara Osei returned to Nairobi after 12 years training and working in Rome's finest kitchens. She had one vision: to bring the discipline and elegance of Italian cuisine together with the bold, soulful flavours of East African cooking.

What started as a 30-seat neighbourhood restaurant in Westlands has grown into one of Nairobi's most celebrated dining destinations, recognised by the East Africa Food Awards in 2022 and 2024.

Every dish on our menu tells a story — of local farmers who wake before dawn, of spice traders at City Market, and of a kitchen team that treats cooking as an art form.

What Drives Us

Our Values

Passion First

Every plate that leaves our kitchen is made with genuine love for food and respect for the guest. We never compromise on quality, no matter how busy the service.

Community Rooted

We source 80% of our ingredients from Kenyan farmers and suppliers. Supporting local businesses is not a marketing slogan for us — it is how we operate every single day.

Always Evolving

Our menu changes with the seasons. Chef Amara and her team travel, experiment, and push boundaries — so there is always something new and exciting to discover at Savoria.

Head Chef

Amara Osei

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Born in Accra and raised in Nairobi, Amara discovered her passion for cooking at age nine in her grandmother's kitchen. At 21 she earned a full scholarship to the Culinary Institute of Rome, where she spent three years mastering classical Italian technique.

After working in two Michelin-starred restaurants in Rome and Milan, Amara returned to Nairobi with a clear purpose: to create food that honours both her African heritage and her Italian training.

Her signature cooking philosophy — "bold roots, refined technique" — is evident in every dish at Savoria.
